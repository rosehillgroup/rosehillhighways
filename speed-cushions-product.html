Applications

Speed cushions provide effective traffic calming across various environments:

Ideal Applications

  • Residential streets with speeding issues
  • School zones requiring speed reduction
  • Car parks and supermarket access routes
  • Logistics depot vehicle management
  • Hospital and healthcare facility approaches
  • Business park internal roads
  • Retail development traffic calming
  • Emergency vehicle routes (allows straddling)
  • Bus route speed management
  • Temporary traffic calming schemes

Colour Selection Guide

  • Black: Professional appearance for urban and business areas
  • Red: High visibility for emergency routes and high-traffic zones
  • Custom colours: Available on request for specific requirements

Height Selection

  • 65mm: Moderate speed reduction for light traffic areas
  • 75mm: Enhanced speed reduction for problematic locations
  • Two-piece options: Available for larger road widths

Benefits Over Traditional Speed Bumps

  • Less aggressive speed reduction
  • Reduced impact on vehicle contents
  • Emergency vehicles can straddle
  • Lower maintenance requirements
  • Faster installation process
  • No excavation required
