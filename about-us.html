About Rosehill Highways

Innovation in Surface-Mounted Safety

At Rosehill Highways, we design and manufacture robust, surface-mounted traffic calming and segregation products that can be installed quickly and effectively without excavation. Our solutions are trusted across the UK and internationally to make streets safer for everyone—whether you're walking, cycling, or driving.

Made in the UK. Installed Worldwide.

All our products are manufactured in West Yorkshire from recycled rubber, helping reduce environmental impact while delivering long-term durability and performance.

We work with local authorities, highways contractors, transport bodies, and international distributors to deliver solutions that are fast to install, cost-effective, and easy to maintain.
