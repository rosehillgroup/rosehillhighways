Product Overview

Rosehill Highways' Narrow Cycle Lane Defenders provide robust, surface-mounted separation between vehicles and cyclists, enhancing safety through either continuous or light segregation.

Designed in line with Cycling England and Sustrans guidelines, our units support the creation of 2.0m-wide cycle lanes wherever possible, with a minimum width of 1.5m.

Manufactured in the UK from 100% recycled tyre rubber, these long rubber units are quick to install, highly durable, and ideal for permanent or trial schemes in urban and suburban environments.

Product Details

Key Features & Benefits

  • Suitable for continuous or intermittent segregation
  • Surface-mounted—no excavation required
  • Fast to install and easily retrofitted into existing schemes
  • Durable, solid rubber construction with excellent impact resistance
  • Units can be swiftly removed and replaced if dislodged or damaged
  • Modular design—extendable to any required length
  • Optional 50mm reflective glass eyes for increased visibility
  • Compatible with a wide range of keep-left signs and reflective bollards
  • Supports cycle bypasses, build-outs, and pinch points

Rosehill Cycle Lane Defenders are engineered for flexible deployment across a range of highway layouts, helping to improve cyclist confidence, reduce incursions, and promote safer cycling as part of modern active travel infrastructure.
