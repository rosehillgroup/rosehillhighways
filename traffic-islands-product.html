Island Types

Our traffic islands are available in one-piece and two-piece configurations, designed to meet the demands of any traffic management situation.

①

One-Piece Islands

Compact units (1m-2m) for quick installation. Solid construction with universal sign plate and underside channelling.

②

Two-Piece Islands

Large units (1.5m-2.4m) supplied in two halves for easier handling while maintaining solid performance.

Heritage Pattern

Available with heritage paver pattern surface for aesthetic integration in sensitive environments.

Extendable Design

All islands can be extended length and width-ways using extension pieces to suit any specification.

Applications

Traffic islands provide effective traffic control and guidance in various highway scenarios:

Traffic Management Applications

  • Junction control and traffic direction
  • Lane segregation at complex intersections
  • Build-out systems for traffic calming
  • Pinch points for speed control
  • Roundabout central islands
  • Highway merge and diverge points
  • Urban street traffic organisation
  • Car park traffic flow management

Infrastructure Integration

  • Support for keep left signs and bollards
  • Mounting platform for illuminated posts
  • Integration with lighting columns
  • Housing for traffic control equipment
  • Cable management through underside channels
  • Battery box integration for powered signage

Choosing the Right Island

  • One-piece (1m-2m): Quick installation, smaller applications
  • Two-piece (1.5m-2.4m): Large control features, major junctions
  • Heritage pattern: Conservation areas, aesthetic considerations
  • Extension pieces: Custom sizes, complex layouts
