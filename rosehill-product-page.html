Product Overview

Our unique one-piece rubber traffic islands are surface fixed in under 30 minutes and offer excellent cost and time benefits compared with constructed, concrete and modular alternatives.

Our one-piece units are available in three widths (1500, 1850 and 2000 mm) and can be installed with the flanged piece (splitter islands) or straight edges. High pressure moulded one is solid unit, these durable islands incorporate a conveyur sign fixing plate and underside channelling enabling them to follow the camber of the road, as well as aid drainage and allow access for cables/conduit if required.

Installation Guide

The one-piece islands can also be used as build-outs, pinch points and pedestrian refuges. Installation is straightforward and can be completed in under 30 minutes:

  1. Prepare the road surface - ensure it's clean and dry
  2. Position the traffic island unit in the desired location
  3. Mark drill points through the pre-formed holes
  4. Drill into the road surface
  5. Secure with appropriate fixings
  6. Install any optional features (signs, reflective eyes)
  7. Complete with road markings as required

Note: Full installation guidelines and technical support are available from our team. Contact us for detailed specifications and site-specific recommendations.
