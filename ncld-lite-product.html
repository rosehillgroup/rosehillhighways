Why Choose NCLD Lite?

The NCLD Lite delivers all the proven benefits of our full-size defenders in a compact, lightweight format that revolutionizes installation efficiency and design flexibility.

Single Installer

At just 24kg, one person can handle and install units safely

Design Precision

Faster Installation

Sustainable Impact

Product Details

Key Features & Benefits

NCLD Lite shares all the core benefits of the full NCLD range while adding unique advantages:

  • Half the length and less than half the weight of standard units
  • Single-person installation capability (24kg units)
  • Enhanced design flexibility for complex layouts
  • Perfect for tight corners and radius applications
  • Surface-mounted—no excavation required
  • Fitted with M24 anchor thread as standard
  • Retroreflective glass eye included
  • Compatible with a range of bollards and signage
  • Quick removal and replacement when needed
  • 100% recycled tyre rubber construction
  • Suitable for permanent or trial installations

The compact size makes NCLD Lite ideal for locations where precise positioning is critical, while the reduced weight dramatically improves installation safety and efficiency.

Applications

NCLD Lite excels in situations requiring precise positioning and flexible installation:

Ideal Applications

  • Tight radius corners and curved cycle lanes
  • Complex junction layouts requiring precision
  • Retrofit projects in constrained spaces
  • Trial schemes requiring frequent adjustment
  • Single-installer maintenance programs
  • School zone temporary segregation
  • Event-based cycle route protection
  • Gap filling in existing installations
  • Heritage areas with access restrictions
  • Quick response safety improvements

Installation Advantages

  • Reduced team size (single installer)
  • Lower transport costs (more units per vehicle)
  • Easier handling in confined spaces
  • Faster installation and removal
  • Improved safety during handling
  • Enhanced precision in positioning

When to Choose NCLD Lite

Select NCLD Lite when you need maximum flexibility, precise positioning, or when installation resources are limited. The compact size makes it perfect for complex layouts while maintaining all the protective benefits of the full NCLD range.
