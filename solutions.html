Cycle Lane Segregation

Protecting cyclists with robust, surface-mounted barriers that create clear separation between vehicles and cycle lanes without permanent infrastructure changes.

How Cycle Lane Protection Works

Our cycle lane segregation solutions create a physical barrier between vehicles and cyclists, dramatically reducing the risk of incursions and improving cyclist confidence.

  • Immediate installation with no excavation required
  • Flexible design accommodates different lane widths
  • High-visibility materials improve driver awareness
  • Impact-absorbing recycled rubber construction
  • Easy removal and repositioning when needed

Perfect for temporary cycle lanes, trial implementations, or areas where permanent infrastructure isn't feasible.

Islands & Refuges

Creating safe spaces for pedestrians and supporting traffic flow with pre-formed rubber islands that install directly onto existing road surfaces.

Smart Traffic Island Solutions

Our traffic islands and pedestrian refuges provide essential infrastructure without the cost and disruption of traditional concrete construction.

  • Pre-formed units ready for immediate installation
  • Support signage, lighting, and street furniture (subject to island type)
  • Improve pedestrian crossing safety
  • Channel traffic flow effectively
  • Weather-resistant recycled rubber construction
  • Reduce vehicle speeds naturally

Ideal for creating pedestrian refuges, supporting junction improvements, and implementing traffic calming measures in urban environments.

Speed Calming

Reducing vehicle speeds and improving road safety through innovative traffic calming measures that work with the road surface, not against it.

Effective Speed Reduction Methods

Our speed calming solutions use engineered vertical profiles and visual cues to naturally encourage drivers to reduce speed while maintaining comfort for emergency vehicles.

  • Progressive speed reduction without sudden jolts
  • Emergency vehicle-friendly design
  • Reduced noise compared to traditional speed bumps
  • Surface profile allows water runoff
  • Installation in under 30 minutes per unit
  • Compliant with DfT guidelines

Suitable for residential areas, school zones, and any location where speed reduction is critical for safety.

Drainage Solutions

Managing surface water with permeable drainage systems that prevent flooding while supporting sustainable urban drainage (SuDS) objectives.

Advanced Drainage Technology

Our specialist drainage division provides comprehensive water management solutions using innovative recycled rubber construction that integrates seamlessly with existing infrastructure.

  • High-capacity modular system manages stormwater runoff
  • Rubber matrix provides partial filtration and sediment retention
  • Supports sustainable drainage requirements
  • Reduces strain on existing drainage systems
  • Made from 100% recycled materials
  • Long-term performance in extreme weather

Essential for car parks, industrial areas, and any surface where water management is critical for flood prevention and environmental protection.
